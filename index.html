Branding
A brand is more than a logo, a color palette, or a nice-looking website. It is the feeling people get when they discover you, the trust they build when they understand you, and the consistency they notice across every touchpoint. We create visual identities and brand assets that make your business easier to recognize, easier to remember, and easier to choose.
Digital Products
Great digital products feel simple, even when the thinking behind them is complex. We design interfaces, flows, and product experiences that help users understand what to do next without friction. From first idea to polished prototype, we focus on clarity, usability, and the small details that make a product feel natural, useful, and built around real people.
Websites
Your website is often the first real impression people have of your brand. It should explain who you are, show what you do, and guide visitors toward action without making them work for it. We design websites that combine strong visuals, clear structure, smooth user experience, and responsive layouts so your online presence feels credible, modern, and easy to use.
Development
Good development is what turns a strong design into a fast, reliable experience. We build websites and digital products that look polished, work smoothly, and perform well across devices. From responsive frontend interactions to clean technical structure, we focus on code that supports the design, protects performance, and keeps the experience stable as your business grows.
Content
Content shapes how people understand your brand. The right words, visuals, icons, graphics, and motion can make your message clearer and your website more memorable. We create content that supports the design instead of filling space, from website copy and visual assets to illustrations, animations, and brand elements that help every section feel intentional.
Generative AI
AI is most powerful when it solves real problems, speeds up smart decisions, and improves the user experience. We use generative AI to explore ideas faster, prototype better interactions, create smarter workflows, and build digital features that feel genuinely useful. The goal is not to add AI for attention, but to use it where it creates real value for your brand and your users.

We design and develop websites that help brands look sharper, move faster, and grow online.

About Us

We are Iskra, a web development and design agency founded in Zagreb, Croatia. We build sharp, conversion-focused websites for brands that need more than a nice first impression. From strategy and UX to design, development, and launch, we turn ideas into fast, polished digital experiences that help visitors understand your value, trust your business, and take action. Every page we create is shaped around clarity, performance, and the goals that matter most: more inquiries, stronger credibility, and better results online.

FAQ

What services does Iskra offer?
We are Iskra, a web design and development agency founded in Zagreb, Croatia. We help businesses turn their websites into stronger sales and trust-building tools through strategy, UX/UI design, branding, responsive development, and conversion-focused landing pages. Our work covers everything from first concept to launch, with every decision shaped around clarity, speed, credibility, and results.
What makes Iskra different from other web design agencies?
We do not design websites just to look good. We build them to help visitors understand your offer, trust your brand, and take action. Our process combines clean design, strong messaging, fast development, and practical conversion thinking, so your website works as a business asset instead of just an online brochure.
Do you work with clients outside Croatia?
Yes. Although we are founded in Zagreb, Croatia, we work with clients across different locations and time zones. Our process is built around clear communication, structured milestones, and focused feedback, so projects stay smooth whether you are local or international.
How much does a website or design project cost?
Every project depends on the scope, goals, number of pages, design complexity, and required functionality. After we understand what you need, we prepare a clear proposal with the recommended approach, timeline, and cost. That way, you know exactly what is included before we start.
Do you work with startups and small businesses?
Yes. We work with startups, growing businesses, and established brands that need a sharper digital presence. If you are launching something new, improving an existing site, or preparing your brand for a bigger market, we can help you create a website that looks professional and makes your offer easier to buy into.
Can you redesign our existing website?
Yes. If your current website feels outdated, unclear, slow, or does not convert well, we can redesign it from the ground up. We look at your structure, messaging, visuals, user experience, and performance, then create a cleaner, stronger version built to generate more trust, inquiries, and business opportunities.
